The trouble with Bears

By Evan Wild

Tall, lumbering, angry, dangerous. Real bears are independent creatures always searching for food.

Types of bear

Bears come as large or medium. Cubs are the only small bears.

Comparison of wild and urban bear characteristics
Bear Type Coat Adult size Habitat Lifespan Diet
Wild Brown or black 1.4 to 2.8 meters Woods and forests 25 to 28 years Fish, meat, plants
Urban North Face 18 to 22 Condos and coffee shops 20 to 32 years Starbucks, sushi

Habitats and Eating habits

Wild bears hunt naturally and live in forests, caves, or mountains.

Urban bears feed mostly on city waste and human food scraps.

Mating rituals

Bears prepare clean and attractive spaces to impress a mate.

About the author

Evan Wild is a wildlife enthusiast from Doncaster.
